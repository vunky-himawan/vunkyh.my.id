**SatuRT** is a web-based information system designed to optimize interaction and management at the Rukun Tetangga (RT) level. The project aims to ease communication between residents and facilitate more effective management of neighborhood data. Using PHP, Laravel 10, TailwindCSS, and AlpineJS technologies, SatuRT offers an intuitive interface and powerful functionality to meet the needs of the neighborhood community.
