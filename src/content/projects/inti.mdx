**INTI** is a web-based information system designed to optimize inventory storage and lending management in the Department of Information Technology (JTI) of Malang State Polytechnic. This project aims to simplify the management of inventory data and inventory loan data at JTI more effectively. Using PHP and Bootstrap technology, INTI offers an intuitive interface and powerful functionality to meet the needs of inventory management at JTI.
