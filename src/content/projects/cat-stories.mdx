**Cat Stories** is a point of sales website used for cat adoption data management. The project aims to simplify adoption data management, facilitate the adoption process, and help find new homes for cats in need. By using NodeJS, ExpressJS, Vite with React, and TailwindCSS technologies, Cat Stories offers a user-friendly interface and robust functionality to meet the needs of cat adoption data management.
